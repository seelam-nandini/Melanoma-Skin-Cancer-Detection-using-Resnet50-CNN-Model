Welcome to Melanoma Skin Cancer Detection Application

This application utilizes the ResNet50 model, a deep learning framework, to deliver precise and reliable melanoma skin cancer detection. By leveraging the latest AI technology, it ensures you receive accurate results that aid in effective health management.

Take the first step towards early cancer detection and empower yourself with proactive care.

To use the application,

  1. Click on the "Upload Skin Lesion" button to upload an image of the skin lesion.
  2. After the image is uploaded, click on the "Predict" button to determine the likelihood of melanoma skin cancer.
